Crab2Go

The Overview

Crab2Go is a family-owned small seafood business developed by a retired and experienced cruise ship chef for 20 years with a strong desire to share his love and passion for seafood.

Our specialty dishes feature fresh crabs and shrimps that are prepared with passion and cooked to perfection in our signature styles and recipes. We make sure that the crabs and shrimps are sourced out daily to ensure the highest level of freshness and quality. They are kept alive 'till it is cooked into perfection right upon each order. Our dishes' taste, smell, and sight will invoke a new sensory experience to fix your seafood cravings!

The Goal

At Crab2Go, we are committed to bringing fresh seafood of excellent quality while sharing a family-focused attitude with a fun, festive, and authentic feel. Whether it is a simple lunch, a romantic dinner, or any celebration, we want to make sure that we satisfy your seafood cravings right in the comfort of your home.

The Logo

We are aware that crab symbolizes the elements of the marine landscape and love for the sea. We developed a simplified version of the crab logo to make it look minimal and straight to the point, creating retention on the customers. It also resonates with the flavors of the dishes that are all made from simple ingredients but combined and cooked in a unique style that will surely satisfy one's cravings for seafood.
