OUR STORY

LET EVERY SIP TELL YOU OUR STORY.

Spirit Bottling Group Inc. (SBG) is a consultant and manufacturer for alcoholic beverages founded in 2020 in South Carolina in partnership with Rock Bottom Distillery. SBG produces and manufactures alcohol-based spirits that cater to the folks who are refined, eloquent, and an overall connoisseur of fine drinks. We specialize in beverages that cater to every on-the-go, free-spirited.

Our name was derived from the dual meaning of "Spirit" - high alcohol content beverages known as "spirits" and the idea that all gifts are given by God and entrusted to us for His glory. The Holy Spirit guides us in these efforts.

We believe that our craft is a never-ending exploration of the senses innate in our thirst for creation and innovation. We craft new experiences that are rooted in tradition. Using the finest grains - we mill, brew, distill, age, blend, and bottle to bring you the highest quality spirits from farm to your glass.

Nothing beats a moment enjoyed with good friends, good music, and a good drink. So, raise your glass, and let us take you on a new journey of drinking experience!
