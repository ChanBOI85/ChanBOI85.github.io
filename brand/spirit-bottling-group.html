Spirit Bottling Group

The Overview

Spirit Bottling Group Inc. (SBG) is a consultant/manufacturer for alcoholic beverages. Working in conjunction with our partner and alcoholic beverage license holder, Rock Bottom Distillery, SBG develops and manufactures alcohol based spirits and Ready-to-Drink beverages.

Spirit Bottling Group was derived from the dual meaning of "Spirit" - high alcohol content beverages ("hard liquor") is known as "spirits". In addition, our company is founded on the idea that all gifts are given by God and entrusted to us for His glory. We are guided by the Holy Spirit in these efforts.

Objectives

  • Brand redesign and identity.
  • Logo redesign.

The Goal

Spirit’s primary goal is to be THE consultant for brand holders on alcoholic beverage manufacturing. And to be in a position where Spirit becomes owner of bottling manufacturing facilities that can supply and sustain brand holders for all their supply needs. From distilling to bottling.

The Logo

We envisioned a classy looking logo for Spirit – our inspiration draws heavily from a bygone era of class and virtue that would represent an air of sophistication that is rooted in the Deep South. The dove is a symbol of spirituality associated with the Holy Spirit with a wheat clutched in its beak. This best represents the state of mind of Spirit Bottling Group grounded in faith and virtue.
